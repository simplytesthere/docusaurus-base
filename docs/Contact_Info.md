## Contact Info

You can submit a ticket via email or phone call 24/7 or drop by the Help Desk during our assistance hours. See below for more information. 

- Email: itshelp@agnesscott.edu
- Phone: 404-471-5487
- ASC Help Desk: 
  - Location: Walters dorm basement
  - Hours: 8 am - 5 pm (pickup/drop-off/assistance hours - 8:15 am to 4:45 pm)
  - Academic Year Open Days: Mon. - Fri. (except college holidays)
  - Summer Open Days: Mon. - Thurs. (except college holidays)


If you have questions or want tips on how to submit a ticket, click this [link](https://asc-testsite2.netlify.app/docs/ticket-submission).
